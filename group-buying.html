Group Buying

Join our group buying events to enjoy premium products at unbeatable prices.

The concept originated over a decade ago, initially supporting cooking and baking communities by providing high-quality products at affordable prices. Since then, it has evolved into a trusted, community-driven platform and a top seller on Korea’s leading N portal.

The group buying process begins with GoMine negotiating supply terms with trusted suppliers. Once terms are confirmed, members place pre-orders for featured products. GoMine then places a collective order, receives the goods upon fulfillment, and arranges delivery or pickup for its members.

We collaborate with trusted suppliers to bring you exclusive deals on limited-time, high-demand items.

Our community-driven model ensures that when more people buy, everyone saves more.
